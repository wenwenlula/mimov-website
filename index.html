Our Philosophy

Movement is personal.
So is your practice.

At Mimov Pilates, we believe that no two bodies move the same way. Rooted in Polestar methodology, every session is built around you — your body, your history, your goals.

This is not a class you attend. It is a practice you develop — one that moves with you, adapts to you, and ultimately becomes yours.

What We Offer
01

Private
